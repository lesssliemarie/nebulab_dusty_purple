Our Mission

We believe that scientists should have tools to manage their data that are as sophisticated as the ones they use to manage their music. Researchers should be focused on research— not digging through their records. The more intelligible and discoverable data is, the more accelerated the research process can be. The future of scientific research depends on a collaborative community around smart, organized data. We’re here to build it.

Your research is complicated.
Managing it shouldn’t be.

Our simple, intuitive interface is modeled after your favorite consumer apps and designed to accomodate your existing workflows.

Intelligible and searchable

Make your data smarter and easier to find by keeping all of a file’s relevant context in one place. Document the details of your experiement with tags, comments, links and attachments.

Share data with your peers

Store and share your data in the same place. Invite collaborators to view and comment on individual files or entire projects with a click of a button.

Use your existing storage

Our platform is currently built direclty over Dropbox, syncing files in real-time from your Dropbox to your Nebulab. You have control of your files, we just make them more intelligent.
